About NuGiBot

NuGiBot is an AI designed to provide individualized personalized nutrition guidance and recommendations. It has several key functions, such as providing food suggestions that match nutritional needs and personal preferences, helping to calculate daily calorie consumption, and providing a log of food and beverages consumed. In addition, the nutrition bot also offers healthy food recipes that match the user's nutritional targets and can be integrated with devices such as smartphones or fitness devices to get more accurate data on physical activity and nutritional needs. Using technologies such as natural language recognition (NLP) and machine learning, nutrition bots can understand user requests and provide appropriate responses, allowing users to get more personalized and effective nutritional guidance without having to consult a nutritionist directly.
